Celebrity Quest

Search for a celebrity that piques your interest.

Wonder what your favorite celebrity is up to?

With Celebrity Quest, you will receive the latest and greatest content about your choice of celebrity. Simply start by typing in the input field and click search. You will see 3 news articles, 3 YouTue videos and some gifs!

Made with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Firebase, Materialize CSS library, and 3 APIs: GNews, Youtube and GIPHY.
